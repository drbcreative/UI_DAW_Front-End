As a unique physician who’s board-certified in both dermatology and psychiatry, Dr. Amy Wechsler treats a full range of skin conditions through her understanding of the mind-beauty connection.

Dr. Amy Wechsler

Dr. Amy Wechsler loves taking care of others. When she was only practicing psychiatry, she helped many; however, being the practical person she is, she wanted to see more perceivable results in her patients. Having noticed even at a young age how one’s feeling state affects how they look, she decided to bridge this gap by combining dermatology with her psychiatry practice. Using the latest technologies and advising the best habits and mindset techniques, she helps people take back control over their lives and skin.

Acne Solutions

Acne is one of the most common skin problems Dr. Wechsler treats for women, men, and teens – and stress and exhaustion are typical culprits. By addressing your well-being and using advanced treatment modalities and technology, she will create a skin-saving strategy for a clear complexion you love. Connect with Dr. Amy Wechsler to get to the root causes of your acne, advise your best treatment options, and ameliorate your acne problems for the long-term.
